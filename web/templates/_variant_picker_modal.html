{# Variant picker modal — opened by a plain client-side button click (no agent/SSE involvement) when a product card's "Add to cart" button is for a product with size/color/material/roast/etc. variants. Usage: a trigger element carries data-product='{"product_id":..., "merchant_domain":..., "name":..., "price":..., "image":..., "option_names":[...], "variants":[{"variant_id":..., "sku":..., "options":{...}, "price":..., "in_stock":..., "image":...}, ...]}' and `onclick="window.__openVariantPicker(this)"`. Submits via fetch POST to /cart/add/{merchant}/{product_id} with variant_id — mirrors the cart-toggle fetch pattern in _chat_sse.html. #}
