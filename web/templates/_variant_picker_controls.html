{# Inline variant picker controls for product_detail.html. Unlike _variant_picker_modal.html (a global overlay opened from a card's "Add to cart" click), this renders DIRECTLY on the product detail page — above the quantity + add-to-cart CTA — since the page is already a dedicated single-product view with no need for a modal. Renders into the #product-variants placeholder. On selection, updates: - the hidden in the add-to-cart form (so it's submitted with the POST), and - the displayed price (#pdp-price) when the matched variant overrides the base price, and - the submit button (#pdp-add-btn): disabled + "Select options" until every dimension is chosen, "Out of stock" if the matched variant is unavailable, otherwise enabled "Add to cart". Only rendered when product.variants is non-empty. #}
